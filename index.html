How Blood Donation Works

Simple steps to save lives

1
Registration

Fill out the donor form with your basic information and medical history.

2
Screening

Medical staff will check your hemoglobin level, blood pressure, and temperature.

3
Donation

The actual donation takes about 8-10 minutes to collect one unit of blood.

4
Recovery

Rest for 15-20 minutes with refreshments before leaving.

Donor Eligibility

Before donating blood, please ensure you meet these basic requirements:

Age between 18-65 years
Weight at least 50 kg (110 lbs)
Hemoglobin level 12.5 g/dL or higher
Good general health
No recent tattoos or piercings (within 6 months)
